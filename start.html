What is Just Intonation?
Just intonation uses simple frequency ratios (like 3:2 for a perfect fifth) instead of equal temperament. This creates perfectly consonant intervals with no beating, resulting in pure harmonic relationships between notes.

Requirements: A MIDI keyboard/controller and a Web MIDI compatible browser (Chrome, Edge, or Opera).
